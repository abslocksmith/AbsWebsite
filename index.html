Van Locksmith Services

All types of van security — slam lock overhauls, full deadlock installs, and mobile onsite fitting anywhere in East London.

Repair

Slam Lock Repairs

Worn brass pins cause locks to stick or fail. We overhaul the mechanism and replace all worn parts.

Install

Deadlock Installation

High-security deadlocks for van side and rear doors — can be matched to your existing slam lock key.

Security

Hook Locks

Excellent resistance against forced entry — hooks lock into the frame, defeating crowbar attacks.

Convenience

Reconfiguring Locks

Want one key for all your van locks? We can reconfigure deadlocks and slam locks to match.

Repair

Burglar Damage Repairs

If thieves have forced your van, we repair the mechanism and restore full security onsite.

Upgrade

Armourshell & Protection Plates

Hardened steel plates fitted over locks to defeat attack tools and protect mechanisms.

Repair

Mechanism Repairs

Central locking faulty? We unlock stuck doors and repair or replace the mechanism.

Security

Additional Van Locks

Extra locks supplied and fitted — ideal for tradespeople carrying valuable tools.

Pedal Box

Secure pedal locks deter vehicle theft even if a thief gains access to the cab.
